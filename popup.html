An autistic user. Pawel is easily distracted when browsing the web.

Pawel's experience

Pawel is easily distracted when browsing the web. You should see images and thoughts popping up, while the original text fades in and out.

Instructions

2. Add the Midnight Lizard extension, which Pawel uses to adjust colors in the browser to help him focus.

Claudia

A sight-impaired user. Claudia uses a screen magnifier to browse the web.

Claudia's experience

There is no automatic simulation for this persona. Claudia changes her settings to view websites at a larger size, with a larger cursor, and higher contrast.

Instructions

1.Create a new Chrome profile in your browser and call it "Claudia". In "Settings > Appearance > Page zoom", select 400% zoom.

2.In your Mac's system settings, navigate to "Accessibility > Display". Set your cursor to maximum size and increase contrast to the maximum.
